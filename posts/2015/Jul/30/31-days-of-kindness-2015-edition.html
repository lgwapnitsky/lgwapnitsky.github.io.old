31 Days of Kindness - 2015 Edition

in 31DaysOfKindness2015  

Last year, after meeting the youngest World Champion of Public Speaking in history, Ryan Avery,I was convinced to join in his first campaign for 31 Days Of Kindness. While I had fun, somehow I slacked a bit and didn’t complete it, but I’m in for a second year.

I’m sure you’re asking, what’s this all about? Well, let me tell you…

(Taken from Ryan’s blog post:)

How it works: Each day we do one act of kindness listed below and use #31DaysofKindness to share, comment and connect with others who are taking part in the challenge.

Make sure to post photos and share updates because we will collect photos and make a HUGE collage to share with everyone who participates! You can also share this with your friends, family, coworkers and neighbors to get them on board! The more we have join, the more good we do as a group! So share away!

Our goal is to retrain our brain to remember to be kind to others on a daily basis and to show the world we can do BIG things when you join together.

Seems simple enough, right? Well, this year, I’m going to commit to the entire 31 days and document it here, as well as on
