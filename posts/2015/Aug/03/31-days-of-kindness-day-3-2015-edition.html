31 Days of Kindness (Day 3) - 2015 Edition

in 31DaysOfKindness2015  

Welcome to Day 3 of #31DaysOfKindness, 2015 edition!

For me, I know I’m sometimes a beast without a cup of coffee in morning. I may be a little addicted, but I can go a few days or more without my “fix”.

Today, I got to make someone happy with a little morning boost of caffeine to feed their own little addiction!

I was running a bit late this morning in order to get to work and take care of today’s act of kindness. I made it out the door and drove the 0.75 miles to the Glenside train station, where one of my favorite neighborhood coffee houses shares space with the ticket booth\waiting area - Elcy’s Cafe!

As I walked in, there was a small crowd gathering, but I made it up to the counter with a gentleman behind me ready to place his order. The wonderful woman at the counter asked me if I wanted to refill my travel mug and, when she asked if I wanted anything else, I turned to the guy behind me and asked what he wanted.

To say that he was stunned is an understatment. He first declined, and I insisted that I buy him his large cup of coffee. He graciously accepted, and paid it forward by putting a very generous tip in the tip jar.

While waiting for the coffee pots to be refilled, we chatted a bit about what I was doing, about #31DaysOfKindness. He was very impressed.

After we each got our coffee, we bid each other a good day, and went off in our own paths. What made this better was seeing the smiles of not only him, but the woman at the counter, and a few other people who witnessed the act of kindness.
