31 Days of Kindness (Days 12 & 13) - 2015 Edition

in 31DaysOfKindness2015  

Today is a two-fer for Days 12 & 13 of the #31DaysOfKindness, 2015 edition. Why a tow-fer? Because I got home too late last night to post!

So, what did I do over the past two days? Well, both days involved helping people in different ways. How? Read on!

Day 12

Today was all about offering my expertise at no cost to someone who needed it. In what turned out to be perfect timing, I was working with my company’s intern (whose last day is this coming Friday). Having worked so many years as a jack-of-all-trades in the IT industry, I felt I could teach him a few things as we were working together throughout the day.

In just one day, we covered a lot of topics. I taught him about:

  • Using Linux-based LiveCDs to do diagnostics on systems, and how many of the utilities I’ve used are no-cost options that sometimes perform even better than the expensive commercial versions

  • How to efficiently search and replace through tons of text using Regular Expressions (a.k.a. RegEx). He was very impressed by that, and even continued with the built-in tutorial that came with another utility that I told him about.

  • Exploring options in the IT world. I’ve covered so much ground from my early days, and it’s taken me in all directions, through many industries, and taught me many new technologies so that I’m never stagnant.

I felt good passing along some of this knowledge. As he’s a sophomore, he may be back next year, and he also has my contact info if he has more questions afer he goes back to school.

Day 13

I think my wife has been waiting for this one…Donate at least one piece of clothing! I admit, I have a ton of clothes I don’t wear, but I’m also very lazy. Well, considering I just bought a few new polo shirts of the right size, and I prefer not to wear cotton shirts, this group of 6 cotton, size Large polos are ripe and ready to be delivered to the New Life Thrift Shop in Glenside, PA.

It’s only a small start, and I’ve plenty to give away. I may put more than this in a bag before I drop it off Saturday morning.

Well, I’m almost 2 weeks in, and I’m feeling pretty good about myself. If you’ve been following along and/or participating in one way or another, please leave a comment letting me know how I’m doing, how you’re doing…whatever!

Stay tuned for more!
