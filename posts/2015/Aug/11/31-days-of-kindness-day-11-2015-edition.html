31 Days of Kindness (Day 11) - 2015 Edition

in 31DaysOfKindness2015  

Day 11 of the #31DaysOfKindness, 2015 edition, REALLY got away from me. I was swamped from early in the morning to late in the afternoon, well after the banks closed. Why are the banks important? Well, today’s act was to place a $2 bill (yes, they still make those) in someplace for someone random to find it.

Considering I was in a datacenter all day, hard at work, I didn’t get to do this. The only other person I really saw was my boss. In a way, that was good, as I avoided this guy…

Nothing I can do about it now, but I may double-down on one of my “my choice” AoK days!

See you tomorrow!
