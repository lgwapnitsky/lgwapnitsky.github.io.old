31 Days of Kindness (Day 1) - 2015 Edition

in news  

As you may have read in the prior installment, I’m participating in #31DaysOfKindess as part of not only improving myself, but helping out others. What was today’s mission? A hand-written thank you note\letter to someone.

Drew Sharkey (Twitter, Facebook) grew up in the neighborhood where I currently live. As a matter of fact, he still lives in the same house in which he grew up. As our Ward Commissioner, he does a LOT for our community, as well as the township as a whole. He’s so popular that, in the last primary election, he received a significant write-in vote from Democrats (he’s a Republican, but don’t hold that against him). He will appear at community events, and takes the time to really get to know his neighbors and listen to their issues. He goes out of his way to be he politician that cares, probably because this area means so much to him.

For that, I felt it was very important to thank Drew for all that he does. Our family is very happy with him as our Ward Commissioner and, as long as he keeps on caring, we’ll keep voting him in.

Thank you, Drew!
